Welcome to Felley Counselling

I'm Joanne, a qualified Integrative Counsellor and Psychotherapist and member of the BACP with a passion for helping people. With 25 years of experience working with people from all walks of life, I offer a unique approach that combines person-centered therapy with attachment perspective and cognitive-behavioral tools to help you overcome challenges such as anxiety, depression, and trauma. Whether you're dealing with a mental health diagnosis or simply looking to understand yourself better, I offer a free 15-minute telephone assessment to help you decide if counselling is right for you.
